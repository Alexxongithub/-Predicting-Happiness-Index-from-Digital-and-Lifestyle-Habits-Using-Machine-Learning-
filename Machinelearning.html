In real social media applications, machine learning is used not just for prediction, but for classification and Natural Language Processing (NLP). These ML models are trained to:

  • Identify Toxic Content - Classify comments and posts as hate speech, cyberbullying, or self-harm-related to automatically flag and remove them.
  • Mood/Sentiment Analysis (NLP) - Analyze the tone of a user's posts to detect potential changes in mental state (e.g., increased anxiety or depression indicators) and trigger proactive well-being resource suggestions.
  • Recommendation Systems - Optimize content delivery not just for engagement, but for "Well-being Metrics" by reducing the visibility of polarizing or comparison-driving content.
